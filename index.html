Our Story

A Legacy of Taste & Elegance

Welcome to Swadhh Restaurant & Cafe, situated in the heart of Rajeev Nagar, Patna. We started with a simple vision: to create a space where exceptional food meets an extraordinary ambiance.

Whether you are looking for a cozy private cabin for a romantic date, a spacious setup for a family dinner, or simply the best Chicken Dehati and Biryani in town, Swadhh is designed to cater to all your culinary desires. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients, blending authentic spices to bring you flavors that linger in your memory.
